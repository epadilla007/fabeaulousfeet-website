The Process

Three steps.
Perfect results.

The Fabeaulous Feet system replaces your entire callus treatment workflow with a cleaner, faster, safer method.

01

Attach a Fresh Disc

Peel a self-adhesive REMOVE disc from the pack and press it onto the stainless steel tip. Two seconds. Guaranteed hygiene for every single client.

02

File with Precision

The 12V electric motor does the work. Gently reduce callus and dry skin without pressure or risk. Sized to fit around and between every toe.

03

Polish to Perfection

Swap to the POLISH disc and smooth the surface to a silky finish. Peel the used disc off. Done. Your client leaves with results they'll rebook for.
