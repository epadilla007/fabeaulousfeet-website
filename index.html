The Process

Three steps.
Perfect every time.

The Fabeaulous Feet system replaces your entire callus reduction workflow. Use no pressure. Gently glide the spinning disc. The RPM does the work.

01

Attach a Fresh Disc

Peel a REMOVE or POLISH disc from the pack. The self-adhesive disc adheres directly onto the Medical Grade Stainless Steel tip. Fresh hygiene for every client — no sterilization guesswork.

02

Gently Glide the Disc

Apply zero pressure. The 12V electric motor and RPM do all the work. The flat surface covers a larger area than barrel-shaped tools and reduces risk of burning the client. Sized to fit around and between every toe.

03

Polish to Perfection

Swap to the POLISH Gray Disc and smooth the entire foot surface. Peel off, discard, done. Your client will say: "OH! My God, my feet have never been this smooth and soft."

Electric File
